Research Interest

 I am working in remote sensing image interpretation and analysis, Change detection; Deep learning; Information Extraction; Spectral unmixing. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Change detection with bi-temporal hyperspectral remote sensing images
  • multi-class change detection
  • Spectral unmixing and analysis
